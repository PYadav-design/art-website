Nike Jordans OG

This artwork is a study in pop art culture, where I highlight my oil pastel skills featuring the Nike Air Jordans 1 against a vibrant nature background. Using a palette of deep reds, bright blues, lush greens, warm oranges, and sunny yellows, I blended colors to capture the essence of these culturally iconic shoes. While applying line mark-making to further elevate it in being creative and iconic to Michael Jordan. The striking visuals reflect both their bold design and their connection to urban culture, dynamically bridging fashion and art, which reflects my skill in designing pop art while being detailed in my art.

City with Potential

This piece is a part of a series of custom cityscape drawings, showcasing my skills in abstract and sketch-style art. The use of geometric shapes and varied perspectives aims to create a very visually engaging and abstract representation of urban environments, through the use of graphite pencils and blending sticks. The shading, line work, and perspective drawing inspiration: The distorted and fragmented appearance is inspired by Cubism and the dynami settings founding comic book art.

Noir Style Glass of Water

This piece is a study of a white charcoal drawing, depicting a glass of water with visible bubbles and reflections against a dark background. The contrast between the bright, detailed glass and the dark backdrop highlights the play of light and shadow, creating a serene atmosphere. Which I implemented through shading, reflection rendering, contrast enhancement with blending the white charcoal. This artwork demonstrates my proficiency with charcoal as a medium and my ability to capture intricate details and subtle lighting effects.

Deadpool Sketch

This sketch is inspired by the 2024 film "Deadpool & Wolverine," capturing the vibrant and chaotic essence of the mercenary. I used a bold color palette infused with bright hues, particularly splashes of yellow, to symbolize Deadpool's playful unpredictability. Through color grading with colored pencils, I created depth and nuance, especially in the lively background. This approach showcases my skills in detailed character illustration and my appreciation for traditional drawing techniques and comic book art. Overall, the piece reflects the energetic spirit of the characters while highlighting my artistic abilities in creating a vibrant sketch.

Aztec Animals

This piece showcases my application of the repousse technique on aluminum, where I skillfully pushed and shaped the material to create designs inspired by various Aztec animals and birds. To enhance the work, I coated the aluminum with black acrylic paint and gently buffed it with a damp cloth, allowing for the intricate details to stand out. This finishing touch not only elevated the piece but also connected it more closely to the rich artistic culture of the Aztecs. Through this artwork, I demonstrate my ability to manipulate physical materials to create truly unique creations.
