Physical Material

Aztec Animals

This piece showcases my application of the repousse technique on aluminum, where I skillfully pushed and shaped the material to create designs inspired by various Aztec animals and birds. To enhance the work, I coated the aluminum with black acrylic paint and gently buffed it with a damp cloth, allowing for the intricate details to stand out. This finishing touch not only elevated the piece but also connected it more closely to the rich artistic culture of the Aztecs. Through this artwork, I demonstrate my ability to manipulate physical materials to create truly unique creations.
