Berlin Postcard

This digital design presents a postcard of Berlin, showcasing my graphic design skills through capturing iconic landmarks in the world. The cityscape highlights notable landmarks and captures the essence of Berlin through an orange-hued artistic lens. While the landmarks remain recognizable, through the application of color grading, photo manipulation, and digital illustration, done in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, the design effectively merges historical and contemporary elements, demonstrating my proficiency with digital tools.

A symbol out of the shadows

This design is inspired by the 2022 film, The Batman, and explores the contrast between lurking in the shadows and confronting harsh realities. The dark figure set against a vivid red background symbolizes the struggles many individuals face when they hide their true selves. I emphasized applying light and dark gradients while creating a paper effect on the design through Adobe Photoshop to further elevate the meaning. This piece showcases my skills in graphic design, demonstrating my proficiency in digital design and composition through 2D elements in Adobe Photoshop.
